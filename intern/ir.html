How to write an SOP?

-Be specific


● Why are you interested in this field?
● Highlight your previous research/project works.
● Mention courses (maybe online).
● Describe your future goals.
● Specifics about the department/ institute you find compelling.


Selection Criteria


(Ranked by importance)
1.CGPA (8.5+)/ Class Rank-Top 5% (depends)
Get your transcript
2.SOP/ LOR
3.Previous projects/ research experience/ CV
4.PORs/Extracurriculars (Mostly not considered)
Things to keep in mind!
●Don’t mail professors already associated with any internship programmes.
●Can concentrate on IITs with no such internship programmes.
●Timeline: start mailing by November end.

Ways to get a Research Internship in India

●Applying through internship portals.
Time frame: Nov-March


1. Summer Research Fellowship Programme (SRFP), IAS
2. JNCASR
3. SPARK, IIT Roorkee
4. SRIP, IIT Gandhinagar
5. SURGE, IIT Kanpur
6. Eklavya Summer Internship, IIT Bombay
7. IIT Hyderabad Summer Internship
8. Tata Institute of Fundamental Research (TIFR)
9. Narendra, IISc


● Personally mailing the professors.


1. Select your university and institute
2. Analyze the professor’s profile
3. Read his publications and research work
4. Write an email (content of the email, next slide)
5. Wait for the response
6. Put a Gentle Reminder
7. If positive, good to go. Else repeat the steps.

Why apply for a research internship?

● Realize whether you are interested in research.
● Helps you develop a few skills and helps to build your profile.
● Not easy to get a development based intern (for 2nd years).
● Better way to spend your summer.


Nature of the internships:



● Application oriented, not theoretical
● Some development component
● Flexible timings (weekly review)
● Basic stipend/facilities
● Final Report (may result in publications too)
